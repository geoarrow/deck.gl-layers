Static pageLoaded

pageLoaded: Promise<void>

Get a 'lazy' promise that resolves when the DOM is loaded.

Note

Since there may be limitations on number of load event listeners, it is recommended avoid calling this function until actually needed. I.e. don't call it until you know that you will be looking up a string in the DOM.
